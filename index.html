Our Mission

We connect you to the resources that help you pass your computer science classes at Tufts University.

Background

This platform was created in 2020 by a Tufts CS students. After everything was moved to online, various professors adapted to the situation and introduced so many tools. Some courses website can be overwhelming. Thus, our founder organized various resources that he found helpful when he was taking various cs class.

We are constantly updating the platform, and we will continue to add more class. If you find any other resources that you think we should add here, never hesitate to share with us.
