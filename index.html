Welcome to TheBrige

Our Mission

We connect you to the resources that help you pass your computer sciences classes at Tufts University.

Background

This platform was created in 2020 by a Tufts CS students. After everything was moved to online, various professors adapted to the situation and introduced so many tools. After feeling overwhelmed, our founder organized various resources that he found helpful when he was taking various cs class. Therefore, he wanted to share them with Tufts community so that they don't have to go through the same tough experience he had.
